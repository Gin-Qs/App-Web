# PRODUCT.md — Fleeter Soluciones Logísticas

## Register
**Brand** (primary public surface = marketing/landing). The app portal at `/app` is a
separate product surface; this file governs the marketing site.

## What / Why
Fleeter is professional cargo transport in Mexico run like a technology company.
The market is dominated by informal trucking: no invoice, no insurance, no GPS, no
visibility. Fleeter exists to change that — formal, traceable, communicative freight.
Founded in **Toluca, Estado de México** (2026); corridor focus Toluca · CDMX · Puebla,
scaling nationally.

Tagline: **"No somos el más barato; somos el que no te deja colgado."**

## Target users
- B2B shippers: central de abasto / distribuidores de perecederos, alimentos con cadena
  de frío, agroindustria, manufactura ligera que necesita trazabilidad y cumplimiento fiscal.
- Buyer mindset: risk-averse, burned by informal carriers, value reliability and
  visibility over lowest price.

## Value proposition (why we win)
1. Trazabilidad en tiempo real (GPS, link compartible, ETA).
2. Comunicación proactiva — **7 puntos de contacto por viaje**; nunca preguntas dónde va.
3. Soporte excepcional — **ejecutivo de cuenta dedicado**, WhatsApp + teléfono directo
   (this is a core differentiator, give it weight).
4. Facturación completa — CFDI + Carta Porte en 24 h, 100% deducible.
5. Seguridad documentada — GPS, candados satelitales, botón de pánico; **seguro de carga
   opcional según el cliente** (NOT mandatory).
6. Cadena de frío con monitoreo de temperatura en vivo.
7. Reportes mensuales de cumplimiento.

## Brand personality (3 words)
Confiable · Tecnológico · Cercano. Professional but warm; "tú" not "usted"; short, direct
sentences; honest about problems, fast with solutions.

## Visual identity (locked — do not reinvent)
- Accent: **Naranja Fleeter `#E65100`** on charcoal (dark default) / white (light).
- Type: **Poppins** (display) + **Inter** (body).
- Logo wordmark: "Fleet" + orange "er".
- Dark + light themes with a toggle. Live tracking is the signature motif.

## Anti-references (avoid)
- Generic SaaS-cream landing, AI-purple gradients, gradient text, glassmorphism-everywhere.
- Emoji as iconography, fake-precise metrics / invented client counts, fabricated logos.
- Eyebrow-on-every-section, numbered-section scaffolding, em-dashes as flourish.
- Looking like every other freight site (stock-truck-on-highway-only).

## Accessibility
WCAG AA contrast minimum, `prefers-reduced-motion` honored for all motion, keyboard-navigable.
Spanish (es-MX) copy and formatting throughout.
